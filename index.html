About Me


I have a B.S. in Computer Science from Rutgers with a Minor in Physics. I also have an M.S. in Information Systems Management. Some of my work experience is in the financial sector where I performed various systems analysis functions for firms such as Citibank and UBS Financial. After that, my father and I ran an auto repair shop and dealership specializing in classic European automobiles. After taking some time off to get married and raise a family, I've rejoined the workforce by accepting a position as data quality analyst for an online auto parts retailer, Ultrarev, Inc. Some of my current responsibilities include maintaining an inventory of over 700,000 auto parts and accessories through various sales channels including the company website, ultrarev.com, as well as eBay and Amazon.


I am eager to build working, useful sites from the ground up, alongside knowledgeable people, because doing so will be powerful, not only for the knowledge gained, but also for the confidence and new ideas that will be birthed as a result.
